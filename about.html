About this project
Jhoom Jingle is under underdevelopment with a scope of many new features. As nothing is perfect everything has posibilities of getting better. The few of the existing features are:

Songs

  • Contains more than 100 song.
  • Features of play and pause.
  • Instant play on click.

Karaoke

  • Browse song from your device.
  • Create Karaoke.
  • Download Karaoke.

Playlist

  • Each playlist contains different set of songs.
  • New playlist can be added by user.
  • Can play any song from any playlist.

Scope
  • More songs
  • Custom playlist
  • Rateing songs
  • Algo to create playlist according to users most played songs
  • Karaoke competition between two users
